StudySteve Privacy Policy

Your data. What we collect, why, and how long we keep it.

Effective date: March 13, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 13, 2026  ·  Operator: Devin Hickman

1. Overview

StudySteve ("the Bot", "we", "us") is a Discord bot designed to help students manage their academic work. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect when you use the Bot, how we use and protect it, and your rights regarding that data.

📌 We do not sell your data to anyone. We do not run ads. Your data exists solely to provide the service to you.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the Service. Here is an exact breakdown of every piece of data stored in our database:

Data type What it is Why we collect it
Discord User ID Your unique Discord numeric ID (e.g. 123456789012) Links all your data to your account. Never contains your username or email.
Discord Guild (Server) ID The numeric ID of the Discord server where you ran commands Keeps your data separate per server so you can use the bot in multiple places.
