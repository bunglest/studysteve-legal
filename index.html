📖 Photosynthesis — Plain English
Photosynthesis is how plants make food from sunlight. Leaves absorb light and use it to convert CO₂ and water into glucose and oxygen. Think of it as a solar-powered kitchen — sun is the energy, CO₂ and water are the ingredients, glucose is the meal. 🌱
