StudySteve Privacy Policy

Your data. What we collect, why, and how long we keep it.

Effective date: March 13, 2026  ·  Last updated: March 13, 2026  ·  Operator: Devin Hickman

1. Overview

StudySteve ("the Bot", "we", "us") is a Discord bot designed to help students manage their academic work. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect when you use the Bot, how we use and protect it, and your rights regarding that data.

📌 We do not sell your data to anyone. We do not run ads. Your data exists solely to provide the service to you.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary to operate the Service. Here is an exact breakdown of every piece of data stored in our database:

Data type What it is Why we collect it
Discord User ID Your unique Discord numeric ID (e.g. 123456789012) Links all your data to your account. Never contains your username or email.
Discord Guild (Server) ID The numeric ID of the Discord server where you ran commands Keeps your data separate per server so you can use the bot in multiple places.
Assignments Subject name, task title, due date, completion status, completion timestamp Powers /add, /list, /today, /done, and all assignment views.
Study sessions Subject, duration in minutes, start and end timestamps Powers /timer and /stats. Shows you how long you've studied.
Grades Subject name, assignment name, score, max score, calculated percentage Powers /grades add, /grades list, and AI grade analysis.
User preferences School name, school portal URL, timezone, morning DM time, weekly report day/time, last message send dates Powers /setup, morning DMs, and weekly reports.
Premium status Whether you have Premium, who granted it, and when it expires Controls access to Premium features.

We do not collect your Discord username, email address, password, IP address, or any financial information. Payment details are handled entirely by Stripe — we never see your card number.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Display your assignments, grades, and study stats when you run commands.
  • Send your configured morning DM and weekly report at the right time in your timezone.
  • Verify your Premium status before allowing access to AI features.
  • Pass your inputs (e.g. "explain photosynthesis") to the OpenAI API to generate AI responses.

We do not use your data for analytics, advertising, or any purpose other than operating the Service.

4. Third-Party Services

Discord

The Bot operates within Discord. Discord's own Privacy Policy applies to your use of the Discord platform. We access your public Discord profile only to read your display name and User ID.

OpenAI

Premium AI features (explain, quiz, flashcards, plan, math, molecule, grade analysis, and schedule import) send your text input or image to the OpenAI API. OpenAI processes this data in accordance with their Privacy Policy. By using Premium features, you consent to your inputs being processed by OpenAI.

We recommend not entering sensitive personal information (passwords, ID numbers, financial details) into AI commands.

Stripe

Subscription payments are handled by Stripe. When you subscribe, Stripe collects your payment information directly. We never see or store your card details. Stripe's Privacy Policy applies to all payment processing.

5. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored in a SQLite database on the server running the bot. Access is restricted to the bot operator. We apply the following security practices:

  • The database file is not publicly accessible.
  • We do not log command contents beyond what is necessary for error debugging.
  • Discord User IDs are stored as text strings, not linked to any external identity systems.

No security system is 100% impenetrable. In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users via Discord as quickly as possible.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as you use the Service. Specifically:

  • Active data (assignments, grades, preferences) — retained indefinitely while you use the bot.
  • Completed assignments — retained so your progress history is available. You can delete them with /delete.
  • Grades — retained until you clear them with /grades clear.
  • After account deletion request — all your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — Request a summary of all data we hold about you.
  • Deletion — Request complete deletion of all your data. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
  • Correction — Most of your data can be corrected directly through bot commands (/setup edit, /grades clear, /delete, etc.).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 9.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for users of all ages, including students. We do not knowingly collect any additional personal data from users under 13 beyond what is described in this policy. If you believe a child has provided data to us inappropriately, please contact us for immediate removal.

Users under 13 should obtain parental consent before using the Service, in accordance with Discord's own Terms of Service which require users to be at least 13 years old.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. We will make reasonable efforts to notify active users of material changes via Discord announcements.
